01 Lead Generation
AI-Powered Lead Qualification & CRM Pipeline

A digital marketing agency was manually reviewing 200+ weekly inbound leads — 12+ hours lost on data entry, scoring, and follow-ups, causing slow responses and lost deals.

Built an n8n workflow that captures leads via Tally, uses Gemini AI to score and classify intent, auto-creates HubSpot contacts and deals, assigns to the right rep, and sends a personalised follow-up — all within 3 minutes of submission.

02 Client Operations
Automated Client Onboarding System

A consulting firm spent 3–4 days manually onboarding new clients — sending contracts, collecting documents, setting up folders, and scheduling kickoff calls, with steps regularly missed.

Designed a workflow triggered on "Deal Won" in HubSpot that auto-sends contracts, creates a Google Drive folder, shares an onboarding form, schedules a Calendly meeting, and notifies the team on Slack — zero manual steps.

n8n HubSpot CRM Google Drive Calendly Slack
3 days→ 20 mins
100%Step completion
03 Reporting
Automated Weekly Business Report

An e-commerce owner spent every Monday pulling data from 5 different tools to build a performance report — 4–5 hours of manual work before the week even started.

Built a scheduled n8n workflow that pulls data from Shopify, Google Ads, and Google Sheets every Monday at 7am, formats an AI summary using Gemini, and delivers a structured Notion report and Slack digest automatically.

n8n Notion API Google Sheets Gemini API Slack
4hrsSaved weekly

04 HR Operations
HR Employee Onboarding Workflow

An SME HR team relied on manual email chains to onboard new hires — missed IT setups, unsigned policy documents, and chaotic first days were routine.

Created a multi-step n8n workflow: new hire data triggers IT ticket creation, auto-assigns equipment, sends a welcome email sequence, schedules training sessions in Google Calendar, and tracks everything in an Airtable HR dashboard.

n8n Airtable Gmail Google Calendar Slack
ZeroMissed steps
90%HR time freed
05 Customer Support
AI Customer Support Triage & Ticket Routing

A SaaS startup's support team had one cluttered inbox — every email was manually read, categorised, and assigned. Response times exceeded 24 hours and team morale was low.

Deployed an n8n + Gemini workflow that reads inbound emails, classifies urgency and category (Billing, Bug, Feature, General), auto-replies to simple queries with relevant answers, and creates prioritised HubSpot tickets for complex ones with the right assignee.
